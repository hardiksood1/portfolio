About Me

I am Hardik Sood, an MSc Data Science graduate from Chandigarh University, with hands-on experience in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Large Language Models. My work focuses on building scalable, secure, and production-ready AI systems across domains such as cybersecurity, healthcare, agriculture, and intelligent video analytics.

I have practical expertise in model optimization and deployment using ONNX, NVIDIA TensorRT, Triton Inference Server, NVIDIA NeMo, and NIM, as well as experience in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), secure ML pipelines, and real-time AI inference.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Explainable AI, and AI security. I am the author of one peer-reviewed research publication in news text summarization and the inventor of five granted patents focused on intelligent automation, safety systems, and smart infrastructure.
